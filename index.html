Cellphone Dock

This cellphone dock is my first and only digital fabrication project. It was developed during the 2016 Spring semester in the course Introduction to Digital Fabrication at the Illinois Institute of Technology. The goal of the device was to make me aware that I was using my cellphone during study/concentration time. This little box produces sounds, send emails and blinks when I decide to check my cellphone while I should be studying.
